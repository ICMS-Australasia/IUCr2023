48 Hours Remains of the IUCr 2023 Early Bird

Registration for the Congress includes:

  • Admission to all Congress sessions 22 – 29 August 2023
  • Admission to the IUCr 2023 Exhibition
  • Delegate materials
  • Ticket to the Welcome Reception held on Tuesday 22 August 2023
  • Ticket to the Congress Gala Dinner held on Monday 28 August 2023
  • Catering including morning tea, lunch and afternoon tea daily as of Wednesday 23 August 2023

Don’t miss your chance to register today and save over $300 on the full registration standard rate!

Rest Easy at the Congress

The IUCr 2023 team have secured 8 accommodation venues, from hotel rooms to apartments to premier rooms; all are near the venue and have first-class facilities for your consideration. Each location provides individual benefits, e.g. close walking distance, swimming and gymnasiums.

It is not compulsory to book through the Secretariat, but if you do, we can guarantee the advertised room rate (inclusive of all taxes). Furthermore, we will provide ongoing support during your stay.

Explore and Tour

Allow time to make the most of your attendance here in Melbourne, home of the 2023 FIFA Women’s World Cup!

The IUCr 2023 Organisers have curated offerings to explore the State of Victoria to ensure your travel to Australia is extra memorable. The Congress will officially commence on Tuesday 22 August 2023 and so we have suggested itineraries that are pre and post Congress for delegates and importantly, for Accompanying Persons to be able to experience the best the State has to offer.
