Fabulous Facets of Melbourne

We want to encourage as much in-person interaction and collaboration as possible, but we also appreciate that there are many factors that prohibit some of Our community from attending IUCr 2023 physically in Melbourne. Therefore we are introducing a virtual registration option for those delegates who would love to be with us in person but can’t make it to Melbourne.

If you are one of the 1,700 registered delegates for the Congress, you will automatically receive access to the Virtual Program asset as part of your registration.

Alternatively, if you register as an in-person delegate between now and the Congress, you will also receive access to the Virtual Program

View and Review

Ahead of the Congress, you will be sent a unique login token by the Congress Secretariat, which will give you access to the content to watch live as it happens or re-watch on demand post-congress.

Can’t make it to Melbourne?
Then we will come to you

Access to the IUCr 2023 Platform and all recorded sessions will remain online up to one-month post-Congress.

Virtual registration entitlements in live streaming and recordings of selected virtual Congress sessions as detailed below:

  • Opening Ceremony
  • All four award Lectures
  • All three Plenary Presentations
  • All Keynote Presentations (30+)
  • 75th Anniversary Symposia
  • Closing Ceremony
  • Access to the digital Poster Gallery

Day Registrations

Can’t attend the full Congress and only have a day or two to spare? A one and two day pass is now available for delegates to purchase. This pass includes admission to the session’s in-person and access to the exhibition on the day of attendance. The pass includes catering on the day of attendance but does not include the Congress social functions.

Industry Workshops

Our industry partners contribute the program with invaluable content and presentation on the latest technologies. Attendance is included in your registration and schedule so they do not conflict with the scientific program.

Dectris’ lunchtime symposium, we will present the key advantages and possibilities of our new, high-end hybrid-photon-counting detector PILATUS4 and how scientific research can benefit from it. We will also show data and results from customers, who have already tested the detector in the laboratory and the synchrotron

Malvern Panalytical will host a lunchtime session on introducing the world’s first compact XRD with exciting new technological advancements, which we are sure will be a session not to miss. If you would like to know more or attend please reach out to us.

Latest innovations from Rigaku Oxford Diffraction will presented by Mathias Meyer and Robert Bücker at their stand. If you want to know what we’ve been up to, please join them on Thursday.

And on Friday 25 August, the new crystallographic platform from Bruker will offer new ways of data processing, information exchange and storage with the potential to revolutionize your daily workflow to improve your success and satisfaction.

You can register for any of the workshops when you register for IUCr 2023. If you are already a registered delegate please email us to secure your place/s and we will add to your profile.
