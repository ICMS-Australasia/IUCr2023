Packing our Pals for Portland

The IUCr 2023 team will be hosting a booth at the American Crystallographic Association 72nd Annual Meeting in Portland, Oregon, from 29 July to 2 August 2022.

We have some special gifts and prizes at stand 310 – make sure you pack your business cards or complete our online expression of interest at the booth. We will need your name and contact if you are one of the lucky winners of the daily prizes!

We are delighted to welcome this month Eldico Scientific as a Diamond Sponsor and Anton Paar as an Emerald Sponsor; their early commitment will provide a layer of additional value for delegates.

A number of companies are in discussion with the team to finalise their involvement with IUCr 2023. We will update you next month. In the meantime, we encourage to you book early to avoid disappointment.

We consider sponsors and exhibitors our collaborative partners. If you or your company are interested in partnering with IUCr 2022, please contact the team on-site at the ACA Portland, or register your online interest @IUCr2023, for a discussion of how best we can support your goals and objectives.

We are preparing for the opening of registration next month, together with the Call for Abstracts. Mark your diaries.

The 12-month countdown begins on 23 August 2022.

Kind Regards

Michael Parker
Chair, IUCr 2023
