Software Fayre at IUCr 2023

As at the previous Congress, the 26th IUCr Congress will once again feature a Software Fayre!

Authors of crystallographic software are now invited to submit an application for a presentation slot to show their new developments. During the presentation authors will be asked to demonstrate the features of their software on one or more practical examples.

The Software Fayre will be held throughout the duration of the Congress with timeslots on Friday 25 August and Tuesday 29 August now open for application.
