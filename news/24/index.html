Golden Nugget Workshops

Dig a little deeper into the IUCr 2023 program and you will find the opportunity to attend one of twenty workshops on offer the two days before the Congress program begins, on Monday 21 August and Tuesday 22 August 2023. You can express interest in these workshops as part of your registration.

Already registered and wish to attend a workshop?
Email registration@iucr2023.org to amend your registration.

Chips off the Old Crystals

IUCr 2023 has developed a frontline STEM project Bragg Your Pattern, which seeks to introduce STEM students and the public to the world of Crystallography alongside the Congress, including holding a crystallographic festival extravaganza, Crystal-A-Con.

Crystal-A-Con, will be filled with seven fun and educational activities, giant crystal structures and patterns on patterns everywhere you go! Hosted at the Melbourne Convention & Exhibition Centre on Friday 25 & Saturday 26 August 2023.

If you are bringing your family, we have on-site day-care for your little chips. Facilities for children, including certified providers/childcare professionals, will be housed at the venue. Parents can reap the benefits of the program and reunite with their children, who are welcome in the exhibition during the breaks for mealtimes. It is part of our commitment to encourage family participation at IUCr 2023.

If you require this support, which is free of charge to attendees, please ensure you register your requirements with the Congress Secretariat.

Bonza Benefits of Attending IUCr 2023

Through our communications, we have endeavoured to give you as many reasons as possible to secure your place at IUCr 2023.

  • By attending IUCr 2023, you will have access to the collective knowledge of leaders in the field of crystallography.
  • You will have the opportunity to meet and connect with over 1,700 of your peers and professionals from across the globe.
  • Contribute and actively participate in your life work.

We also know that attending events of this international standing means travelling for the majority of the attendees. So if you haven’t already registered, or you are considering inviting more team members or colleagues, we trust we have given you a few more reasons to do so.

Crystallising Connections

The IUCr 2023 Opening Ceremony includes a Traditional Welcome Country and the 13th Ewald Prize Lecture by Prof Wayne A. Hendrickson. The Opening Ceremony will then be followed by the Welcome Reception in the Congress Exhibition. This is not your ordinary welcome; there will be entertainment, local cuisine, and fabulous beverages.

Hungry for More?

The IUCr 2023 Congress Party is included in your Full Congress registration. The final celebration of Crystallography Week will culminate at the Congress Dinner – A Night at the Museum.

Hosted at the Melbourne Museum this evening is set to be a highlight of your attendance. If you haven’t spent a Night at the Museum here is your chance. Meander through all the activated spaces including the Dynamic Earth, Science of Life, and the Melbourne Exhibitions.
