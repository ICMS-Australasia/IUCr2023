Full Program Now Available

With over 110 micro symposiums, 30 Keynote Speakers, 17 pre-congress workshops, four prize lectures and three Plenary Speaker presentations, there is so much to get excited about in the IUCr 2023 Congress Program.

Message from the Program Chair,
Brendan Kennedy

Judging by the quality of the submitted abstracts, the International Program Committee has nailed it in terms of scientific interest. I am now working with the chairs of the individual microsymposiums to fill all the available slots, but at this stage, it is clear that IUCr 2023 will offer a strong and diverse scientific program.

A number of prize winners will be presenting their work at the Congress, including Prof Wayne Hendrickson, Prof Jian-Min Zuo and Prof Arkadiy Simonov. Their award lectures promise to be a highlight of the scientific program.

I am also excited to witness our Plenary, Keynote and Invited Speakers across the Congress program, who come from all areas of the crystallography field, and are experts in each of their respective areas. I am also thrilled by the quality of the various abstract submissions that have been accepted as orals and posters for the Congress and look forward to the presentations that are to come.

One of the most engaging and stimulating parts of any IUCr Congress is the poster sessions and our next task is to begin to build this part of the program. If you have not yet submitted an abstract, but would like to present your work as a poster in Melbourne, we are still accepting submissions for poster presentations.

We look forward to all the Program has to offer and look forward to you joining us in Melbourne in August.

Brendan Kennedy
Program Chair

Join us at IUCr 2023

With so much on offer in the program, don’t miss your chance to join us for IUCr 2023!

A reminder that Poster abstracts submissions are still open until the end of this month, 31 May 2023.

Registration gives delegates access to all Congress sessions, social events and Pre-Congress Workshops* as well as daily catering and access to the exhibition.

IUCr 2023 will be the leading event for Crystallographers world-wide, so don’t miss your change to be involved.

Visit Victoria and Marvellous Melbourne

The IUCr 2023 Organisers have curated offerings to explore the State of Victoria to ensure your travel to Australia is extra memorable. The Congress will officially commence on Tuesday 22 August 2023 and so we have suggested itineraries that are pre and post Congress for delegates and importantly, for Accompanying Persons to be able to experience the best the State has to offer.
