Thank you Crystallography Community!

We want to thank and acknowledge all of you who have submitted abstracts to the 26th Congress and General Assembly of the International Union of Crystallography Congress 2023. The enthusiastic response from our global community has met all expectations. We are excited that our goal to create a content-rich programme is coming to fruition.

In light of the multiple requests, we have listened. We have re-opened the Call for Abstracts to allow additional time for those who need it. The submission portal will now remain open until 21 February 2023.

New dates and timelines for the Call for Abstracts can be found on our website.

Submit Now

We do recognise it is a busy time of year for everyone. For North America, we wish you a very Happy Thanks Giving and for those who celebrate this traditional time of year, we wish you all a very happy season. For our most northern colleagues and friends, we send you our warmest wishes as you head into Winter. Wherever you are in the world, we wish you a safe and happy time for the holiday season.

The extension of the Call for Abstracts deadline will be the final opportunity for you to be a part of the IUCr 2023 program.

Still Making the Decision to Travel?

The decision to register and travel is flexible for some months, but some pre-planning will make the journey a pleasant one. The Australian borders are open with zero COVID-related restrictions at this time. We have streamlined the Visa Application process for you – but diarise to apply no later than June 2023.

We have a dedicated page linking directly to the Department of Home Affairs information to answer your Visa questions. The Congress is registered with the Department of Home Affairs International Event Coordinator Network (IECN). Once your register, your name is automatically provided to the IECN as a delegate of IUCr 2023 to make the visa application as smooth as possible. Of course, we urge you to check your home country's policy and monitor the additional Australian border information here.

Australia is even easier to get to these days. Did you know 61 airports around the world have direct flights to Melbourne, spread around 60 cities in 19 countries? Getting around Melbourne is even easier with inner-city tram travel.

A Crystal Legacy

We want delegates to feel comfortable bringing their families along. Everyone is welcome to join us in Melbourne to network and provide a vibrant in-person environment. We are offering on-site childcare facilities and carers to remove some of the worries you may feel about finding a trusted sitter overseas to support you. Let us help you.

The “Froebel Crystal Creche" and the "Little Chips" Fun Zone will be a highlight of the Congress and one we aim to leave a great impression and a Legacy outcome for IUCr 2023.
